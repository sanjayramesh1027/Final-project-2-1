Nutrition Basics

Learn how to fuel your body for optimal health and fitness with these simple nutrition tips.

1. Balanced Meals

Aim to include lean protein, whole grains, and a variety of colorful vegetables in every meal.

2. Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to stay hydrated and support your metabolism.

3. Healthy Snacks

Choose snacks like nuts, fruit, and yogurt to keep your energy levels stable throughout the day.
